                    **Examples:**
                    
‚Ä¢ "Why AI regulation will destroy innovation" ‚Üí 16,632 likes

‚Ä¢ "AI will replace managers before developers" ‚Üí 12,800 likes

#### 2. Loss Aversion (8x Multiplier)

                
                    **Examples:**
                    
‚Ä¢ "95% of AI startups will fail for this reason" ‚Üí 18,500 likes
‚Ä¢ "The AI mistake costing companies $100B" ‚Üí 15,200 likes
